Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Disc Golf Tracker ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Disc golf data: Course names, hole information, scores, and round statistics that you enter into the App
  • Google account information: When you sign in with Google, we access your name, email address, and profile picture for authentication purposes

Information collected automatically:

  • Local storage data: The App stores your preferences and data locally in your browser

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide the disc golf tracking functionality
  • Store your rounds and statistics
  • Sync your data across devices (when signed in with Google)
  • Authenticate your identity

4. Data Storage

Local Storage: Your data is stored locally in your browser using IndexedDB and localStorage. This data remains on your device.

Google Sheets: When you sign in with Google, your data is synced to a Google Sheet stored in your Google Drive. This data is governed by Google's privacy policy and your Google account settings.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your disc golf data is either stored locally on your device or in your personal Google Drive account.

6. Third-Party Services

The App uses Google Sign-In and Google Sheets API. When you use these features, your use is subject to Google's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data at any time through the App or your Google Sheet
  • Delete your local data by clearing your browser storage
  • Delete your synced data by removing the Google Sheet from your Drive
  • Revoke the App's access to your Google account through your Google account settings

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
